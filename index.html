Pepperoni Pizza Recipe

Perfect for pizza nights at home!

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 2 tsp dry yeast
  • ¾ cup warm water
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup pepperoni slices
  • Optional toppings: basil, olives, mushrooms

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. In a bowl, combine warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for 5 minutes.
  3. Add flour, salt, and olive oil to the yeast mixture. Knead into a smooth dough.
  4. Cover and let the dough rise for 1 hour.
  5. Roll out the dough on a floured surface.
  6. Spread pizza sauce, add mozzarella, and top with pepperoni.
  7. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until golden.
  8. Slice and enjoy!
